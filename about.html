This cafe was created out of a love of the Harry Potter movie franchise and all the things inspired by the wizarding world! We created this cafe to bring these movies to life.

This is our story

Beginning of Butterbeers and Pumpkin Pasties

For my final project I will be creating a food company that is based on the foods from Harry Potter movies and the wizarding world. It will be called Wizardly Broomsticks Cafe. It will serve Butterbeer, pumpkin pasties, chocolate frogs, Cauldron cakes and drinks, Treacle tart, fish and chips, and other foods inspired and also mentioned in the movies and books.

Using the app you will be able to make a reservation and also place a catering order for certain selections of the menu and will also include an in depth overview of the menu. The app will be more specific for making a reservation for sit in.
